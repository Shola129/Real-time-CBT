Platform Features

Everything You Need to Run Examinations

A comprehensive suite of tools built for academic institutions, training bodies, and professional certification programs.

Secure Authentication

Role-based access control ensures that admins, students, and examiners each see only what they need, with session management and audit trails.

Organization Management

Create and manage your entire institution — departments, student groups, exam schedules — all from a single, unified dashboard.

Student Self Registration

Students join your organization using a unique code, removing manual admin overhead and streamlining the onboarding process.

Online Timed Exams

Set precise start times, durations, and end windows. The system auto-submits when time runs out, preventing overruns.

Automatic Grading

Results are computed instantly at submission. No manual marking — objective question banks are graded in milliseconds.

Question Bank

Build and organize a rich question bank by subject and department. Reuse, shuffle, and randomize questions across exam sessions.

Result Management

Review, approve, and publish results with one click. Students receive notifications the moment results go live.

Performance Analytics

Track student performance over time with visual dashboards — spot weak areas, measure improvement, and generate reports.

Responsive Design

Works flawlessly on desktops, tablets, and mobile phones. Students can sit exams from any device with a modern browser.

How It Works

Up and running in four simple steps

From account creation to published results — the entire workflow is designed to be intuitive for administrators and students alike.

Create Organization

Get Organization Code

Students Register

Students sign up using the organization code, creating their profiles and getting access instantly.

Take Exams & Get Results

Students sit scheduled exams and receive automatic results the moment they submit — no waiting.
